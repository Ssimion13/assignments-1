Boy Falls In Mud Puddle!

Much to the delight of multiple onlookers, a boy named Johnny fell in a mud puddle near Chestnut and Main Street on Tuesday. Two city employees working nearby attempted to pull Johnny out of the puddle, but were unsuccessful on their first attempt.

They narrowly excaped being pulled in with him as he fell back into the puddle. They were both, however, soaked from the splash made by his second fall. Undetered, the cityworkers were eventually able to extract Johnny from the puddle.

Multiple witnesses described the incident as the funniest thing they'd ever seen. Mrs. Mable Bartholemew exclaimed, "My stars! I thought I'd never stop laughing!" As for poor Johnny, except for his pride, he was uninjured.
